# **App Name**: MediChain

## Core Features:

- EMR with SOAP notes, CPOE, clinical decision support, vitals trends, care plans: Core Clinical & Care
- e-Prescribing, barcode eMAR, pharmacy inventory with expiry/batch tracking: Core Clinical & Care
- Lab (LIS), Radiology (RIS/PACS), Pathology workflows with result alerts: Core Clinical & Care
- OR scheduling, surgical safety checklists, anesthesia & post-op records: Core Clinical & Care
- ED triage (ESI), fast-track, observation units: Core Clinical & Care
- Inpatient bed dashboard, nurse tasks, discharge planning: Core Clinical & Care
- Inventory: reorder alerts, procurement, supplier management: Admin & Operations
- Facility: bed status, housekeeping, dietary orders: Admin & Operations
- Real-time dashboards: ED wait times, bed occupancy, OR utilization, lab TAT: Analytics & Safety
- Quality: incident reporting, infection surveillance, audits: Analytics & Safety
- KPIs: ALOS, readmission rates, claim denials, patient NPS: Analytics & Safety
- RBAC, audit logs, MFA, encryption, consent management: Security & Compliance
- HIPAA/GDPR-ready, HL7/FHIR/DICOM interoperability: Security & Compliance

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Calm blue (#72BCD4) to evoke trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) for a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Muted green (#A5D6A7) for positive cues and important actions.
- Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif, for clear and accessible text rendering in records and general display.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, a contemporary headline font that aligns to medical industry standards of legibility.
- Use consistent, professional icons for various medical functions and data points.
- Prioritize clean and efficient layouts for quick access to critical patient information.
